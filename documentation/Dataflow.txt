Frontend → Kong → EasyAppointments (booking request)
                    ↓
              Call Pricing Service API
                    ↓
          Pricing Service queries its DB for:
          • Market median prices
          • Historical surge data
          • Vendor surge preferences
                    ↓
          Returns: recommended_price, surge_multiplier, breakdown
                    ↓
         EasyAppointments displays price to customer
                    ↓
              Customer confirms → Booking created
                    ↓
              Celery job triggered: market_collector (daily)
                    ↓
              Market Collector updates pricing DB
                    ↓
              Next booking request gets updated market data
